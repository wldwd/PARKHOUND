About ParkHound 🐾

Hey there! We’re ParkHound – your go-to map for sniff-approved parks around Perth and beyond. We built this so you can:

  • Discover off-leash areas (fenced or open)
  • Check amenities like water taps, bins & shade
  • Get contact & website info for each dog park

Whether you’re chasing fetch dreams or just vibing with your pup on a sunset stroll, ParkHound’s got your back. We’re all about making doggy adventures easy, fun and safe.
