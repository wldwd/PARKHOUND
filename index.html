How to use this map

Dog parks appear as purple areas on the map. Click on any purple area to see details about that dog park and get directions.

To find dog parks in a specific area, type a suburb name or local government area into the search box on the left, then press enter.

The map will zoom to your chosen location and highlight the dog parks there in pink.

A list will also appear with the names of parks in that area. Click on the names to see them highlighted in fuschia on the map.
